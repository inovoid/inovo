Highlight Heater

Engineering Product Design
Client: Hanil
Project focused on reimagining the traditional highlight (radiant infrared) heater. Standard quartz and halogen heaters are often visually aggressive, industrial, and glaringly bright. The objective was to design a highly efficient, directional heating monolith that acts as a sleek, architectural addition to premium residential and hospitality spaces.
